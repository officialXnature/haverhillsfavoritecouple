Pink Pussy

A scandalously pink libation that whispers "I have assets" while obnoxiously proclaiming to be from Haverhill. Classy enough for The Roma; scandalous enough for The Candlelite.

  • — 1.5 oz Baja Rosa strawberry cream liquor
  • — 1 nip Dr. McGillicuddy's Menthol Peppermint Schnapps
  • —Make it squirt with a splash of Cherry Bitters

Nichols St. Negroni

One sip and you’ll be bouncing off the walls like you just stumbled accross someone's stash. Equal parts baking soda and “no, you cant find this in the liquor store,” this Negroni here will have you questioning your life choices... while constantly peeking out the window.

  • — 1 gram Coke
  • — pinch of Arm & Hammer
  • — spoon cooked to perfection over a low flame
  • — smoked (optional)

Lemno-Cello

For those who think lemoncello tastes like Pine-Sol with a passport blessed by someone’s abuela who used to throw olives at bad suitors. You tried lemoncello once... That was enough character development for one lifetime. Please cease and dezest.

  • — 1.5 oz citron vodka
  • — nothing lemony
  • — 0.5 oz rosemary syrup
  • — garnished with a lemon wheel safely out of arm's reach

Plastered in Plaistow

Missed the packy in Mass?? Head up Route 125 for this border-town favorite. You wont be taxed but bring plenty of bail money.

  • — 1 oz dark rum
  • — 1 oz blanco tequila
  • — 0.75 oz lime
  • — 0.5 oz cane syrup
  • — garnished with a DUI

Tequila Mockingbird

Part Southern Gothic, part seaside siesta. The kind of drink you bring to a book club if you want everyone to gossip and then forgive you by dessert.

  • — 2 oz reposado tequila
  • — 0.5 oz mezcal (smoky wink)
  • — 0.75 oz hibiscus-lime syrup
  • — lime & toasted salt rim

Mai Tai'd the Knot

Perfect for newlyweds and people who just want to promise things under tiki-torch lights. A citrusy vow with a rum aftertaste that says, "We're not getting any younger."

  • — 1.5 oz aged rum
  • — 0.5 oz curaçao
  • — 0.75 oz lime juice
  • — 0.5 oz orgeat & 0.25 oz simple

Ex on the Beach

Rum, regret, and a little pineapple. Best enjoyed while looking at the ocean or at someone's expired relationship status with envy.

  • — 1 oz white rum
  • — 1 oz peach schnapps
  • — 1 oz cranberry
  • — splash pineapple & lime
  • — garnished with radish

Plum Island Iced Tea

A coastal long island remix. Salty breeze, sweet berries, and the faint possibility of a tan.

  • — 0.5 oz vodka
  • — 0.5 oz gin
  • — 0.5 oz rum

Rum Around & Find Out

The Cutoff

  • — 0 oz vodka
  • — 0 oz triple sec
  • — 0 oz lime juice
  • — garnished with great lighting and a faint scent of dishrag
